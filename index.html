About

I am a Full-stack Developer with hands-on experience building real-world web applications across both front-end and back-end. I have worked in freelance, part-time, and internship roles, contributing to products from development to deployment.

On the front end, I specialize in React, Next.js, and TypeScript, focusing on performance, scalability, and user experience. I have experience using Storybook and shadcn/ui to build and standardize reusable UI components, ensuring maintainability and consistency across projects.

On the back end, I have worked with PostgreSQL and MySQL, built RESTful APIs, and used Strapi as a headless CMS. I also have foundational experience with Apache Kafka for asynchronous data processing. In addition, I have experience with PHP (CodeIgniter) and WordPress, including e-commerce projects and legacy systems.

My goal is to build scalable, maintainable products and create meaningful value through clean, efficient code.
